MAIN INTERESTS

Ms. Agnès Trudeau is particularly interested in the evolution of family law in a context of profound societal changes. She closely follows legislative reforms and case law developments in matters of filiation, parental authority, and protection of vulnerable persons.

Sensitive to amicable approaches to resolving family conflicts, she is trained in family mediation and favors, when possible, negotiated solutions that preserve family ties.

On the academic side, she regularly participates in conferences on family law and contributes to specialized legal publications. She is also involved in associations that help families in difficulty.
