MAIN INTERESTS

Mr. Gabriele Direnzo is particularly interested in intergenerational transfer issues and the impact of new family structures on inheritance law. He closely follows the evolution of European law in succession matters.

Passionate about history, he is a member of an association for the preservation of Burgundy's architectural heritage and participates in restoration projects of historical buildings in the region.

On the academic side, he regularly participates in continuing education programs for notaries and lawyers on the topics of inheritance taxation and wealth transfer strategies.
