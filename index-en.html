About Our Firm

A legal reference for over 25 years

Founded in 2000, Perrin & Associates has established itself as an essential reference in the legal and notarial field. Our firm brings together exceptional lawyers and notaries, all driven by the same passion: defending your interests with excellence and rigor.

Our philosophy is based on three fundamental pillars: expertise, proximity, and innovation. We take pride in staying at the forefront of legal developments while maintaining a privileged relationship of trust with each of our clients.
